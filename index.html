Hello there. Here's me and here's what I'm working on!


Welcome to the website I'll be using to host my writing works as I release them. This place is subject to change in time as I get better with web design and other creative ventures. For now, it's pretty basic. You likely know me by Fenn Unsu, Labyrinth, or Hunter. I hope to keep your attention with my writing. I am very open to constructive criticism on my works as well, if you have any make sure to give me a reach out via the contact section at the bottom of this page.



Currently I am working on 'To these I welcome along, Sun, Moon, and Star.' (Referred to as SMS VN or SMS:A internally. A fanwork of 'Sun, Moon, and Star. One of which is untitled and unreferable, whilst the other is a side project to accompany a very early game development project some will know as 'Objects Floating in the Wind' (OFitW).



I have a number of internal project ideas that are not fully expanded on, though I hope to get to a point where I am comfortable sharing these as well. Thank you for your time, and I hope the journey to be worth it.

Breaktime Chats with Anelise

Current Status: Finished

This was a short story created in a week or so for one of Epic Department's writing contests. This story won me the writing contest and awarded me some rather wonderful constructive criticism. I want to do more with the characters in the work, but for now here they are in a really short romance story about two adults at work.

To These I Welcome Along, Sun, Moon, and Star.

Current Status: Ongoing

This is a fanwork for Cyn_Clical's Roblox game Sun, Moon, and Star. To These I Bid Adieu. It follows my own personal fancanon and uses characters outside of where they should be. This also features several different characters unique to the fanwork itself and aims to create a story told as if I were the one who wrote the lore of the game, while appealing to fans of several different characters. This is my main currently ongoing project.
